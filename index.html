why you choose petspital!

Video calling

You can arrange an online appointment with registered doctors through our app. The doctor will check your pet via video conferences and examine the symptoms and complications. Then, they'll advise you and recommend blood tests or any lab diagnosis if required. 

Ask Question

You can post any queries on our forum section and our doctors will check it within a short time. Then they'll provide a solution for your pet. In addition, other pet owners are available there to share first-hand experiences which will be very helpful for your journey with pet buddies. It's like a friendly, beautiful community.

Home Visit

If the online consultation is not effective anymore, a physical checkup has become a must; you can request a home visit to your vet. A vet will visit your house to ensure a proper examination. Also, emergency medical help at home can be delivered by our app. 

Medicine

A convenient feature of petspital is ordering the suggested medicines and other critical pharmaceutical necessities for your pets through our online med store. You'll find the most authentic drugs at a reasonable price.The thing you want is now one click away! 

Vacination

Find what vaccines are available in Bangladesh, and any vaccination center is open nearby for your furry babies. Also, learn about diseases that require vaccination, how many doses need to be shot, and the estimated price.
